PRIVACY POLICY

Effective Date: 3/1/2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bates Hotel. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting any personal information you share during your stay or visit to our website. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email, phone number, and payment details when booking a room.
  • Usage Information: IP address, browser type, and interaction with our website.
  • Stay Information: Check-in/check-out details and any special requests.
  • Security Footage: For safety purposes, select areas of the motel are monitored.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Processing bookings and payments.
  • Communicating about reservations or motel policies.
  • Improving our services and website.
  • Ensuring guest safety and compliance with motel policies.

(Mother also likes to keep track of who stays the night.)

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or share your information with third parties, except:

  • Legal Requirements: If required by law enforcement or authorities.
  • Security & Safety: To protect our guests, staff, and property.

(Some records are kept... indefinitely.)

5. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

Our website may use cookies to enhance your experience. You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but some features may not function properly.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect your information. However, no system is completely secure.

If you feel your data has been compromised, contact us immediately.

(Though, some things are best left undiscovered...)

7. Your Rights & Choices

You can:

  • Request access, correction, or deletion of your personal data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Disable tracking features through your browser settings.
