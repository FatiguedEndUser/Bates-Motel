About Us

“Some places have history. Others have secrets. Here at Bates Hotel, we have both.”

Welcome to Bates Hotel, a family-owned establishment where hospitality runs in the blood. Every guest deserves a personal touch because at Bates Motel, you’re not just checking in, you’re becoming part of something... special.

Whether you’re passing through or looking for a quiet escape, we ensure a comfortable, unforgettable stay—the kind that lingers in your mind long after you’ve left.

But don’t worry… Mother takes care of everything.

BATES HOTEL FAQ

General Questions

Q: Where is Bates Motel located?
A: We are nestled in the quiet town of White Pine Bay, offering a peaceful and private escape.

Q: What are your check-in and check-out times?
A: Check-in is from 3:00 PM, and check-out is by 11:00 AM.

Q: Do I need to book in advance?
A: While walk-ins are welcome, we recommend booking in advance—some guests decide to stay longer than expected.

Rooms & Amenities

Q: What types of rooms do you offer?
A: We offer Standard, Deluxe, and Suite accommodations, each designed for comfort... and quiet.

Q: Do the rooms have Wi-Fi and TV?
A: Yes, all rooms include free Wi-Fi and a TV for your entertainment. Just don’t stay up too late...

Q: Are pets allowed?
A: Unfortunately, we do not allow pets. Some things are better left unseen at night.
